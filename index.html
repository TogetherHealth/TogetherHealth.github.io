FAQ

Together Health is a return to "old fashioned" medicine. It is a direct financial arrangement between patients and their physician...no insurance companies! By becoming a member, patients receive all the professional services offered by the physician in the comfort of their own home or via telehealth for a monthly (or yearly) subscription fee. As an added bonus, patients receive wholesale prices on lab tests and medications.

Although Together Health does not accept insurance, there should be no impact to your plan. You should be able to use your health insurance to care provided outside of Together Health such as (Labs, Vaccines, etc.)

The benefits you receive from your insurance company are determined by the insurance company. They should honor any lab or diagnostic testing, prescriptions and referrals ordered; however, that doesn't mean that they make it easy for patients and physicians. We encourage all patients to familiarize themselves with their coverage benefits as insurance companies routinely require "prior approval" or "prior authorizations" for routine diagnostic testing and services. We will do our best to find specialists to meet your needs that are covered by your health insurance company and will assist in the prior authorization processes they require. As an alternative, we have partnered with like-minded companies to offer affordable lab testing and medications. We are also building collaborative relationships with multiple specialty physicians to ensure that you can receive the care you require, regardless of the changing insurance company rules.
